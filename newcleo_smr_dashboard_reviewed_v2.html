Baseline compiled from EIA Today in Energy, 27 Apr 2026, then layered with NRC ADAMS, DOE/national lab releases, company releases and selected industry press through April 2026.

Post-EIA updates implemented: FANCO / EAGL-1, Hermes 2 groundbreaking, ACU MSR-1 MWt correction, Eielson AFB, and Janus candidate-site handling.
Investor-use caveat: This is a market-intelligence map, not a licensing-status register. Capacity, fuel and deployment dates should be treated as developer-stated unless independently confirmed.

Coordinates are approximate to street address where available, otherwise city/site centroid. All markers link to Google Maps.
